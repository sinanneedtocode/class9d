To Class 9-D

❝

From first day jitters to final day tears, we've been through it all together. Every laugh we shared, every challenge we faced, and every memory we created has made this year special. We may have been the class that gave teachers headaches, but we were also the class that stuck together through thick and thin.

To all the friendships formed, the inside jokes created, the study sessions that turned into gossip sessions, and the countless moments that made our year memorable - thank you. Whether we were pranking teachers, struggling through math, or celebrating our achievements, we did it as one.

As we move forward, let's carry these memories with us. Here's to 9-D - the class that wasn't just a class, but a family of mischief-makers, dreamers, and friends for life!
